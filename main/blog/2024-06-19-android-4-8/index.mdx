**[Update Now!](https://play.google.com/store/apps/dev?id=8483587772816822023)**

Thank you for your continued support of OsmAnd. Happy navigating!

## New terrain colorization

New colorizations of color scheme of [terrain](https://osmand.net/docs/user/plugins/contour-lines#color-scheme) were added.

Now user can create and added own color palette for Terran. Just compose your txt-file with next names `height_altitude_name`, `hillshade_color_name`, `slope_name`:

*height_altitude_0-200.txt*

```
# 0 - 90 degree RGBA
0,46,185,0,191
# yellow 
100,255,222,2,227
# red
200,255,1,1,255
# violet
220,130,1,255,255

```

After move the txt-file to _..Android/data/net.osmand/files/color-paletee/height_altitude_0-200.txt_

Next, new palettes will appear on Colour scheme menu:

![Palettes](./img/palette.png)
