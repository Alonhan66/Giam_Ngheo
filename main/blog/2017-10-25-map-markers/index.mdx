<i>Map markers</i> are points on the map you need short-term. A shop you've planned to visit today, an event location you'll only need once, etc. While favorites are the points you'll need in a long run, map markers are created and removed on the go.

<!--truncate-->
 
## Map markers for Android

<p>To start using them, just make a long tap on the map and select the flag icon in the context menu. Done. Now, this point is your map marker. All of them are stored in the <i>"Map markers"</i> menu in OsmAnd.</p>

- <i>"Plan route"</i>.

<p>Now you can plan the route using your markers. Open Map markers > Options > Plan route. Select the markers that you need. You can also start a route from your current location. Drag markers up or down the list to change their order. Here you can also choose the route mode: map overview, car profile, cycling or pedestrian mode.</p>
<p>Then press 'Options' again. Here you'll have several alternatives: </p>

- <b>Make round trip</b>. Use this option if you'd like to return to your initial location.
- <b>Sort by</b>. Here you can pick where you'd like to create the most optimal route using 'Sort door-to-door' option or 'Reverse order' if you'd like to reverse the order of waypoints on your list.

<p>Now you're ready to press 'Navigate'.</p>

- <i>"Save as new GPX track"</i>. Export all your markers at once as a GPX track.
- <i>"Move all to history"</i>. Press it, when you do not need the markers anymore.

<p><b>Markers from favorites</b></p>
<p>Create markers from your groups of favorites easily! Simply go to My places > Favorites > choose your group > press the 'flag' button > select which points to add and press 'flag' again.  Your favorites have been added as map markers. You can also add GPX waypoints as markers. Just go to My places > My tracks > choose a track > Points > press the '⋮' button > Add to map markers.</p>

<p><b>Indication</b></p>
<p>There are 3 ways to view information about your map markers' location in distance to them: a widget on the top of the screen, arrows pointing to the direction of your markers or guidelines.
To manage these options, please go to Map markers > Options > Distance indication.</p>
<p>You can also pick how you'd like to view the information: as a top bar on the map screen or a compact widgets in the upper-right corner. If you do not need this info, you can disable it by choosing 'None'.</p>
<p>To change the order of your map markers, you can drag them using the '≡' button.</p>
