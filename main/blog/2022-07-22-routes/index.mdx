There are thousand official and nonofficial travel routes which symbols (marks) you can see in cities, forests and etc. All these routes are travel routes for your activities. ## Routes in OsmAnd

In OsmAnd you can highlight routes (present on [OpenStreetMap](https://www.openstreetmap.org/)) and [hiking symbol](https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Trail_markings) overlay for your activities. It is very useful for your cycling, hiking, etc. You can select multiple routes at once though they will be displayed as layers and if the road has multiple routes over it only top colour will be visible. If you have some specific rendering styles enabled, you could have a bigger routes variety i.e. for Snowmobile style you could select Snowmobile routes.
